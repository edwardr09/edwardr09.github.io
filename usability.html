Usability Study

Method

I studied my own portfolio as my website. My target audience was other students from different classes. I had them browse the website and perform tasks with Javascript functions and see if there were any issues.

Results

My subjects completed their task easily with minimal problems. There was a problem with the navigation bar, but nothing that prevented them from not being able to do the task.

Discussion

I learned that there were some things in my website that I either forgot or realized were problems, etc. I think that this activity will help me and my website in the future.
