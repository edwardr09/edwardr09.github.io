Evaluation of Web Graphics

Site evaluated: Nike

Purpose of graphics on this site:

The graphics on the Nike front page are used as a visual to help consumers get an idea of what the company is selling. There are several images and visuals on the front page showing different aspects of the brand. Additionally, the front page uses alignment and balance as a way to help make the images on their site more appealing. There is no specific banner that is supposed to hook you in, but rather several different images that'll show its audience how diverse Nike is.

Guidelines for effective use of graphics

  1. Yes - Graphics fit the purpose, organization, and style of the page.
  2. Yes - Large graphics have been avoided.
  3. Yes - Graphics help guide the viewers' focus to the important content on the page.
  4. Yes - There are no overly bright or obnoxious images.
  5. Yes - Avoids the use of graphics to convey textual content information
  6. Yes - Provide textual equivalent alternatives for graphic content
  7. Yes - sufficient contrast between the text and the background color
